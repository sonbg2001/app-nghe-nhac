Harry Styles

Harry Edward Styles (born 1 February 1994) in Worcestershire, Redditch, England, is an English singer, songwriter, actor, and former member of the boy band One. Direction. He started his music career as a singer for a small group called White Eskimo in his hometown of Chapel, Cheshire. In 2010, Styles auditioned for the British television show The X Factor. After being disqualified as a solo artist, Styles was recalled to the contest, along with the other contestants, to form the group One Direction. Niall Horan

Niall James Horan (born 13 September 1993) is an Irish singer and songwriter, best known as a member of the boy band One Direction.[1] In 2016, Horan signed a recording contract as a solo artist with Capitol Records. In 2010, Horan auditioned as a solo artist for the seventh series of the British film singing competition The X Factor. He failed to progress on the "Boys" genre at the "judges' homes", but after judge Nicole Scherzinger suggested that Niall join One Direction, with Harry Styles, Zayn Malik, Liam Payne and Louis Tomlinson, the group became a reality. The five-member group made it to the final round and came in third place. Although they did not win, One Direction was signed by Simon Cowell to Syco Records. The band has released five commercially successful albums, performed on four world tours, and won "This Town" and "Slow Hands", which are included on his upcoming album. During his career as a member of One Direction, Horan co-wrote many of the songs for all five of their albums, including the singles "Story of My Life" and "Night Changes". He also plays guitar for Take Me Home.

After being eliminated as a solo singer, he auditioned again in 2010 and was put in a group with four other contestants to form One Direction. Since then, the group has released five albums, embarked on four world tours, and won several awards. Payne has worked with other producers under the monikers "Big Payno" and "Payno" creating remixes of his band's songs and for Cheryl's. On January 13, 2016, after nearly 6 years of activity, 1D announced the group would be shutting down. After that, Payne began her acting career. Payne signed a recording deal with Republic Records in North America. In May 2017, Payne released "Strip That Down" as the single from her upcoming debut album.
